About Skill Power Institute

Our History

Skill Power Institute (SPI) is a non-stock, non-profit learning organization committed to community service through quality education. Established on November 4, 1997, SPI became a pioneer in tertiary computer education, not only in Antipolo but also in neighboring towns and municipalities.

Mission

To provide accessible and high-quality education that empowers students with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in their careers and contribute to society.

Vision

To be a leading institution recognized for excellence in education, innovation, and community service.

Core Values

Integrity: Upholding honesty and ethical standards in all our endeavors.

Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in education and professional growth.

Community Service: Making education accessible and impactful for society.

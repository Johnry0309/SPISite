Enrollment Requirements

Requirements for New Students

  • Form 138 (Report Card)
  • PSA Birth Certificate (Original & Photocopy)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • 2x2 ID Pictures (4 copies)
  • Long Brown & White Envelopes
  • Filled-out Registration Form

Requirements for Transferees

  • Transcript of Records (TOR) or Certified True Copy of Grades
  • Certificate of Transfer/Honorable Dismissal
  • PSA Birth Certificate (Original & Photocopy)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • 2x2 ID Pictures (4 copies)
  • Long Brown & White Envelopes
  • Filled-out Registration Form

Requirements for TESDA Programs

  • High School Diploma or ALS Certificate
  • PSA Birth Certificate (Original & Photocopy)
  • Barangay Clearance
  • 2x2 ID Pictures (4 copies, white background)
  • Medical Certificate (Fit to Work)
  • Filled-out Registration Form

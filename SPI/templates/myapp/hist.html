Our History

Skill Power Institute (SPI) is a non-stock, non-profit learning organization committed to community service through quality education. It was established on November 4, 1997 and has become one of the pioneers in tertiary computer education, not only in Antipolo but also in neighboring towns and municipalities.

Following its success in Antipolo, SPI expanded its reach by establishing various branches. Nego-Skwela was opened in Quezon City, and AMS and CDC courses were introduced in locations such as Quezon City, Angeles, Cabanatuan, Urdaneta, Tarlac, and Sucat.
